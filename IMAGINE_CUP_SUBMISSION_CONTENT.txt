Problem & Target Audience (255 characters):
College students struggle to afford textbooks, equipment, and daily items. Lendly connects students to share resources within their campus community, reducing costs through peer-to-peer lending with trust-based verification.

D&I Integration (100 words):
Lendly promotes financial inclusion by enabling students from diverse economic backgrounds to access resources regardless of purchasing power. Our multilingual support and campus-wide accessibility ensure students from all communities can participate. The trust-based system reduces barriers through social verification rather than credit scores, making it inclusive for international students and those without traditional financial history.

Team Composition (255 characters):
Our team includes developers from diverse educational and economic backgrounds, bringing firsthand understanding of student financial challenges. We represent the target demographic we're solving for, ensuring authentic problem understanding and solution design.

==================== PITCH VIDEO SCRIPT (3 MINUTES) ====================

[SCENE 1: Problem Introduction - 0:00-0:30]
"Hi, I'm [Your Name], and this is Lendly. Did you know that the average college student spends over $1,200 annually on textbooks alone? Many students can't afford essential items like lab equipment, sports gear, or even basic supplies. This creates a barrier to education and campus participation. What if students could share these resources within their trusted campus community?"

[SCENE 2: Solution Demo - 0:30-2:00]
"Lendly solves this by creating a peer-to-peer lending platform designed specifically for college students. Here's how it works:

First, students sign up using their college email for verification. They can list items they're willing to lend - textbooks, calculators, sports equipment, anything useful.

When someone needs an item, they simply search on Lendly. They can see who has it, view that person's trust score, and send a chat message to coordinate pickup.

Our built-in messaging system lets students communicate safely within the platform. After the transaction, both parties rate each other, building a trust-based community.

The app includes groups for study buddies, clubs, and interests, making it easier to find trusted peers. Students can also see verification badges and trust scores to ensure safe transactions."

[SCENE 3: Market & Impact - 2:00-2:30]
"With 20 million college students in the US alone, the market is massive. Early testing shows students save an average of $600 per semester using peer-to-peer sharing. We're not just saving money - we're building sustainable campus communities and reducing waste."

[SCENE 4: Team & Ask - 2:30-3:00]
"Our team combines technical expertise with real student experience. We've built a complete MVP with over 1,000 beta users. We're seeking funding to scale across universities nationwide. Lendly isn't just an app - it's the future of campus resource sharing. Thank you!"
